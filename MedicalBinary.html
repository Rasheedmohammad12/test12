About Us

Medical Binary Establishment - Specialized in Integrated Medical Solutions

At Medical Binary, we provide comprehensive medical solutions including everything needed for orthopedic surgeries, including implantable materials from international agencies like Austofix (Australia) and Ortho-Trauma (England), as well as a full range of orthopedic surgical instruments, external fixation systems, various medical devices, hospital supplies, and operating room equipment.

In addition to a comprehensive drug warehouse stocked with pharmaceutical and nutritional supplement agencies from around the world, we also have our own pharmacy selling medical products and medications.
