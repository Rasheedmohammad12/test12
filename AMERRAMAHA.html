About Us
At Medical Binary, we provide comprehensive medical solutions including orthopedic surgery supplies, implantable materials from international agencies like Austofix (Australia) and Ortho-Trauma (England), as well as a full range of orthopedic surgical instruments, external fixation systems, medical devices, hospital supplies, and operating room equipment.

We maintain a comprehensive drug warehouse stocked with pharmaceutical and nutritional supplement agencies from around the world, plus our own pharmacy selling medical products and medications.

We also provide healthcare services for international patients, facilitating their medical treatments through our collaboration with doctors, hospitals, pharmacies, laboratories, radiology centers, and other healthcare institutions in the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan.
